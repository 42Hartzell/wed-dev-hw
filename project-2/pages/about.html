A little about me & this Instructable

We love baking at my house. It feels warm and cathartic. Music is turned on, ingredients are brought to the butcher block and the process begins. A lot of the time, my son bakes with me and this gives us a chance to be immersed in food and spend time with one another. Something about food, disarms people and get them to open up. I feel that dialogue is always the best when surrounded by food.
